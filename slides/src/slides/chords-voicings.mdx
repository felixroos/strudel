# chord symbols vs voicings

```js
{
  m7: ['3 7 10 14', '10 14 15 19'],
  7: ['4 9 10 14', '10 14 16 21'],
  '^7': ['4 7 10 14', '10 14 16 19'],
}
```

Example: 

```
Cm7 = C3 (48) + m7 ([3, 7, 10, 14] or [10, 14, 15, 19])
= [48+3, 48+7, 48+10, 48+14] or [48+10, 48+14, 48+15, 48+19]
= [51, 55, 58, 62] or [58, 62, 63, 67]
```
