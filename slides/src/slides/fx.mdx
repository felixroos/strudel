# audio effects

- filters + reverb + stereo effects:

<SlideRepl
  hideHeader
  canvasHeight={200}
  fontSize={24}
  log
  tune={`note("[c a f e](3,8)".sub(24))
  .sometimes(add(note(12)))
  .s("sawtooth")
  .lpf(sine.range(300,4000).slow(16))
  .lpa(0.005).lpd(.1).lps(.2).lpr(0)
  .lpq(12).ftype('24db').lpenv(4)
  .release(.25)
  .room(1)
  .juxBy(.5,rev)
  .gain(.5)`}
/>

- delay with multiple effect sends (orbits):

<SlideRepl
  hideHeader
  canvasHeight={200}
  fontSize={24}
  log
  tune={`stack(
  s("bd <sd cp>")
  .delay(.5)
  .delaytime(.33)
  .delayfeedback(.6)
  .orbit(1)
  ,
  s("hh*2")
  .delay(.8)
  .delaytime(.08)
  .delayfeedback(.7)
  .orbit(2)
).sometimes(set(speed("-1")))`}
/>
